## A HEALTHY BABY BEGINS WITH YOU

## PREGNANCY &amp; ALCOHOL DO NOT MIX

Drinking beer, wine or liquor while you are pregnant or a nursing mother can be harmful to your baby.

According to the U.S. Surgeon General, no amount of alcohol consumption can be considered safe during pregnancy.
